# MedixHealth

## Profile
Integrated hospital network — 14 hospitals, 62 clinics, 1 specialty cancer center. HIPAA-regulated. CIO-led IT with a strong Chief Medical Information Officer counterpart who owns clinical system decisions jointly.

## Engagement History
- 2024: [[medix-emr-2024]] — EMR modernization to FHIR-native platform, all clinics cut over at M+11.

## Relationship Notes
Every change that touches clinical workflow requires a Clinical Safety Officer sign-off on a CSR (Clinical Safety Report). Budget this into timelines — a full CSR cycle is 3 weeks minimum. Prefers partners with healthcare-specific references; see [[integration-risks]] for the HL7 v2 patterns recurring across their estate.
