# NovaRetail

## Profile
Multi-brand retail group with three consumer brands — sports, fashion, home — operating DTC online plus 220 physical stores. Digital accounts for 41% of revenue and growing. CTO reports to COO; digital product org sits inside merchandising rather than IT.

## Engagement History
- 2024: [[nova-commerce-platform]] — 9-month headless replatform. Came in 4 weeks ahead of plan.
- 2025 (forecast): store-ops mobile rewrite — pursue-go decision pending.

## Relationship Notes
Product-led decision culture — RFCs over RFPs. Procurement follows engineering; do not try to push a proposal through procurement first. Decisions move fast; proposals older than ~10 business days tend to lose.
