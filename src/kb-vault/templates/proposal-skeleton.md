# Proposal Skeleton

## Purpose
Top-level structure for any solution proposal. Reuse with care — each section has a recommended length in business days of effort for a seasoned team.

## 1. Executive Summary (0.5d)
*One page. Problem, approach, why us, commercial shape. No tech jargon.*

## 2. Understanding of Requirements (1–2d)
*Restate the client's goals in our words. Call out assumptions explicitly.*

## 3. Proposed Solution (3–5d)
- Architecture (reference [[hld-microservices]] or a domain-specific HLD template)
- Tech choices justified by delivery track record — link to relevant project notes, e.g., [[acme-core-banking]], [[nova-commerce-platform]].

## 4. Delivery Approach (1–2d)
- WBS (see [[wbs-banking-migration]] for banking; adapt per domain)
- Team shape and ramp
- Governance and reporting cadence

## 5. Relevant Experience (0.5d)
*Three case studies max. Pick the ones most analogous to the client's situation.*

## 6. Risks & Mitigations (0.5d)
*Pre-populate from [[integration-risks]]. Add 2–3 client-specific risks.*

## 7. Commercials (1d)
*TCO model, fixed-price vs T&M split, assumptions, change-control mechanism.*

## 8. Why Us (0.5d)
*Differentiators anchored in the case studies. Keep it crisp.*
